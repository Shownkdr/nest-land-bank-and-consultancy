Loan rules at a glance

  • Eligibility: Individuals 18+ with steady income; income-tax payees (including agriculture participants).
  • Loan Tenure: Up to 30 years, must be repaid before borrower reaches 70 years. For multiple borrowers the youngest earning co-borrower's age is considered for max tenure.
  • We support reimbursement for own investments made within the 12 months preceding purchase/repair/construction.

Services & Use Cases

  1. Purchase a plot for building a house (Realty).
  2. Purchase/Construct a new house/flat.
  3. Purchase existing (old) house/flat or extend an existing house.
  4. Repair or renovate an existing house/flat.
  5. Takeover/balance transfer of home loans from other banks/HFCs/FIs.
  6. Reimbursement of investments made from own resources during the preceding 12 months for purchases/repairs/construction.
